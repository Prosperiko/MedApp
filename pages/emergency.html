When to Call 911

Cardiac Emergency

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Loss of consciousness

Stroke Symptoms

  • Face drooping
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech difficulty
  • Sudden confusion

Severe Injury

  • Heavy bleeding
  • Broken bones
  • Head trauma
  • Severe burns

Allergic Reaction

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Swelling of face/throat
  • Rapid pulse
  • Dizziness or fainting
